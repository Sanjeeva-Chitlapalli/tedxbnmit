About TEDxBNMIT

TEDxBNMIT 2022

A student body club started in the year 2015, with backbone faculties Chaitra N and Vaishnavee Manaay with our predominant features retaining the purpose and vision of TED. As a club, we pursue to reach out to as many as people with the sole purpose to engage and share knowledge and experiences from all parts of the world

Events

TEDxBNMIT 2022

TEDxBNMIT is the annual techno-cultural festival of BNMIT organized by its students. TEDxBNMIT attracts the best technical talent to compete with each other and prove their competence. TEDxBNMIT brings creativity and freshness in its events by introducing at least one new event every year to bring in new challenges among the participants, the fest culminates with a Mega Event. We have a lot of events lined up, about which we will brief you shortly. Sit back and enjoy the show, we have a lot to share.
